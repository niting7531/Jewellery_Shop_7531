Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the lucky draw?

Any customer who makes a purchase of $100 or more from our jewellery collection during the promotional period is eligible to participate in the lucky draw.

How do I register for the draw?

After making your purchase, simply click on "Enter Draw" and fill out the registration form with your details and receipt number. You'll receive a unique ticket number via email and SMS.

Can I enter multiple times?

Yes! Each purchase of $100 or more qualifies for one entry. You can make multiple purchases to increase your chances of winning.

When will the draw take place?

The grand lucky draw event will take place on December 31, 2026, at 6:00 PM at our Luxury Jewels Showroom. You can attend in person or watch the live draw online.

How will winners be notified?

Winners will be announced during the live draw event and will be contacted via phone and email. Winners' names and ticket numbers will also be displayed on our website.

How do I claim my prize?

Winners must claim their prizes within 30 days of the draw date by visiting our showroom with valid ID and their ticket number. Prizes cannot be exchanged for cash.

Is the draw process fair and transparent?

Absolutely! We use a certified random selection algorithm, and the entire draw process is conducted transparently during the live event. All entries have an equal chance of winning.
